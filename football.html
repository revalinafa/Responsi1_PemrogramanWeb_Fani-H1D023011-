Football

About Football

Football is the most popular sport globally, played by two teams of 11 players with a spherical ball. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing goal.

Football Rules

The game lasts 90 minutes, split into two halves of 45 minutes each. The game is officiated by a referee and two assistant referees, ensuring fair play and adherence to the rules.
