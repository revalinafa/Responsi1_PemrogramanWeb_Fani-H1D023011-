About Basketball

Basketball is a dynamic and fast-paced team sport, played by two teams of five players each. The objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoops.

Basketball Rules

Games are typically played in four quarters of 12 minutes each. Players must dribble the ball while moving, and the game includes techniques like shooting, passing, and rebounding.
